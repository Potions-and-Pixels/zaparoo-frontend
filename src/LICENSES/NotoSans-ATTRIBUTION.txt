Noto Sans Font Attribution
==========================

This software bundles Noto Sans font files under resources/fonts/:

  resources/fonts/NotoSans.ttf
  resources/fonts/NotoSansArabic.ttf
  resources/fonts/NotoSansDevanagari.ttf
  resources/fonts/NotoSansHebrew.ttf
  resources/fonts/NotoSansJP.ttf
  resources/fonts/NotoSansKR.ttf
  resources/fonts/NotoSansTC.ttf
