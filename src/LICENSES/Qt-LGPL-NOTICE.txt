Qt Modules Used
---------------
The application directly links Qt Core, Qt GUI, Qt QML, Qt Quick,
Qt Quick Controls 2, Qt Quick Templates 2, Qt Quick Layouts, and Qt Svg.
Desktop deployment may also copy supporting Qt libraries/plugins such as
Qt Network, Qt OpenGL, Qt QmlModels, Qt DBus, platform plugins, image format
plugins, and QML module plugins required by the selected Qt installation.

Static MiSTer Qt builds are configured to use Qt's bundled copies of zlib,
libpng, libjpeg, FreeType, PCRE2, and HarfBuzz where selected by
Dockerfile.toolchain. These components are brought in through Qt's build and
remain under their upstream licenses as documented by Qt's own source tree and
license-attribution tooling.
